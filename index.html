Learn

IncubateDFW will be an educational experience for participants of all levels. We will have various workshops from which participants can learn entirely new skills or platforms, and many mentors who will be able to share their knowledge acquired with years of experience.

Build

IncubateDFW will foster growth, creating an environment in which participants can create revolutionary products with technology. With 30 hours to hack, teams will have the ability to complete polished products with the potential to disrupt industries.

Connect

Because IncubateDFW is an entrepreneurship hackathon, participants will have diverse skillsets. Designers, developers, and budding entrepreneurs will come together to network and create amazing products.

When/Where?

IncubateDFW will take place on August 6-7 at the Dallas Fort Work.

Is there transportation or travel reimbursement?

TBD.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop, toiletries, a sleeping bag, and any other technology that you want to hack with.
You don't need a predetermined team or a product idea.

.
Who can apply?

Any high school or college student can apply to participate in IncubateDFW, regardless of experience with programming. Because it is an entrepreneurship event, we are looking for a diverse pool of participants in terms of skillsets.

How do I apply?

Just fill out the registration form! More info will be sent out later.

What is the application deadline?

This will be decided later. The sooner you apply, the better!

How much time will we have to hack?

Teams will have 30 hours to build their products.

Will there be hardware or API keys provided?

Yes. We will release a list of hardware and API's later.

Do I need to be able to code?

No! Participants will be able to act as product managers or designers for their teams, or play other roles. If you don't know how to code but want to learn, you can attend one of our various workshops.

How much does the event cost?

It's free! We will also provide meals, snacks, and refreshments throughout the event.

Is there a Code of Conduct?

Yes, here.
tl;dr respect other participants and don't be a jerk.

Will there be prizes?

Yes. We will have both physical prizes and opportunities with sponsoring companies. A full list of prizes will be released later.
